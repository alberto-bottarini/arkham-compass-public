Dreams of the Clay

Skill Neutral

Innate. Augury.

Artist, Clairvoyant, Dreamer, Performer deck only. Commit only to a skill test on a treachery. If this skill test is successful, put Dreams of the Clay into play in your play area instead of discarding it. When you draw a non-peril treachery from the encounter deck, remove Dreams of the Clay from the game: Cancel its revelation effect and discard it.
